#Circularity #US 

The Commons is a website, and App, where you can learn about how sustainable brands are, and look for sustainable items using some different filters based on your biggest priority (i.e: Water Usage, Labour, Carbon impact, Organic etc ....)



![[Screenshot 2025-06-12 at 15.38.42.png]]


> [!NOTE] A Sustainability Shop Assistant
> Commons really is a shop assistant, with rating of brands, articles, and a reward system to make it worth your while. 
> It also allow you to dig more into general brands. While the evaluation is not as deep as Green Digest would for instance, it is a good indicator and starting point for your purchase decisions.
