#Fashion #FoodSystems #CarbonFootprint 

Source: ecologie360 

> Just launched, this partnership enable companies, partners and individuals to check the environmental impact of their clothes. 

> [!NOTE] How does it works
> 
> You get a number. The lower the better. It looks at various environmental impacts: 
> - Material
> - Origin
> - Transport
> - Lifespan
> - End Of Life

Note that it will not be mandatory to put it on labels. so it is voluntary information only. It will supplemeent the PEF (Produt Environmental Footpriint) which the EU is working on 

See https://ecobalyse.beta.gouv.fr/
