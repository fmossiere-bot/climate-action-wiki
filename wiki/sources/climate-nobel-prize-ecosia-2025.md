# Source: Ecosia Pushes for a Climate Nobel Prize

**Publication:** Good Good Good, Kamrin Baker, 11 December 2025
**URL:** https://www.goodgoodgood.co/articles/nobel-prize-in-climate-and-planetary-health-ecosia

## Summary

Ecosia CEO Christian Kroll has notarised a €1 million commitment to fund the first endowment of a Climate and Planetary Health Nobel Prize, and has contacted the Nobel Foundation to explore making it official. The campaign argues that recognising climate leadership at the highest global level would inspire action in the same way the economics Nobel shaped priorities when it was introduced in 1969.

## Key points

- Ecosia committed €1m endowment via notary in Berlin; open to extending support over time
- Proposal: a 7th Nobel category — Climate and Planetary Health
- Three proposed recognition areas: Pragmatic Governance, Scaling Prosperity and Markets, Building Common Ground
- Ecosia would have no influence over nominations or laureates
- Symbolic blue chair crafted and placed at the Nobel ceremony
- Supported by Luisa Neubauer, Dr Claudia Kemfert (energy economist)
- Comparison to economics Nobel introduced in 1969 as recognition of the priorities of that era
- Campaign website: climatenobelprize.org
