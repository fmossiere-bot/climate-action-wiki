#### What about our Holidays to Portugal? 
Ferry: 152kg = 0.152 tonnes
Not easy but found t his article: https://ferrygogo.com/carbon-footprint/ which talks about 19g/km per person. this is 1000km
Car: based on 3500km = 0.89 tonnes

Alternative would have been Flight + rent a car
Flight: equivalent for 4 person: 2.1tonnes (some other calculation gave me 3.2tonnes)
Car:  0.07 tonnes

We, conservatably, save more than 50% of our emissions.
