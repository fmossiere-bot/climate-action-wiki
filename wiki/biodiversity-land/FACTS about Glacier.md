## An interview with Heidi Sevestre

> *“Heidi Sevestre, French “glaciologue” who travels from Groenland to the Alpes, says it is time to explain the public what is going on”* 
> ==*Source: Ecologie360 - #11 Automne 2025*==

Through multiple trips to the Antartic, she now noticed worrying signs of some of the glacier melting. They seemed to escaped for years, but in the last decades that has changed. 

`If the Glacier in ANtartic were to disappear, the Sea Level would rise by 58m`

- Every degree increase humidity by 7%, does that mean more snow fall? We don;t know, this is why studying this region is so important
- The extremes warm up much faster. You can notice up to 40degree difference in some days to normal temperature. We see more green, and animals survive less as a result
