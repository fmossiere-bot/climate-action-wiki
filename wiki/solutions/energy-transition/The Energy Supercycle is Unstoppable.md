**A revolutionary change is underway**—an energy “supercycle” characterized by a profound, long-term shift toward electrification and clean energy generation.

**Electricity will become the foundation of nearly all modern systems,** from transportation to industry to data centers. Central to this transformation is the rise of AI, which presents both a challenge in the form of increasing demand and a solution as it unlocks new possibilities for efficiency and grid resilience.
