## We live in a DIGITAL World

Whether it is a computer for work, entertainment, communicating with the world, video streaming, calculating complex scenario, optimising energy efficiency with AI, our world is digital and interconnected. Let me ask you, how many Connected device do you own? including yes, your speakers, TV, your connected watch etc ... 

Our life is Digital, and we can';t have enough of it

## Digital Transformation for Sustainability
When we think Digital, we might think social media, streaming, work. But Digital Technologies can help us reduce emissions, become more efficient, bring more educaiton and support for those in needs, monitor biodviversity more accurately, reduce water usage and water waste ... the list goes on. 

Digital transformation is more than just scanning paper document and making them easily searchable on our tablet.s.It is about renewing technolgoy, developing it. AI has tremendous opportunity. 1 

For this reason, Digital Transformation is a pivotal instrument to fight climate change, biodiversity loss and pollution. It is estimated that Digital Technology can positively influence 70% of the 169 Sustainablity Developments Goals targets.

At the same time, Digitisation is inherently energy and material intensive, and is fueling unsustainable consumption, exacerbating inequalities  

And so Digital and Sustainability need to go hand in hand. Goals need to be aligned. From a Sustainability lens, we need to make sure that our transformation and both DIGITAL and GREEN.


Digital transformation for Sustainability is about bringing new technologies that can help us reduce our emissions, become more efficient, but also help to bring more education and support to those most in need, access to electricity, water ... 

Digital transformation is more than just scanning paper document and making them easily searchable on our tablet.s.It is about renewing technolgoy, developing it. AI has tremendous opportunity, but it also create new challenges with the power required to generate.

As the digital word, one where we spend 1/3 of our life on screens consuming news, entertainment, social feeds, audio and more ... present many challenges, and unchecked digitalization can accelerate the drivers of the Triple Planetary Crisis: climate change, nature loss and pollution. But we can avoid that future. If YouTube reduced unused images and started audio-only streaming for videos of just music, they could save 500,000 tons of CO2. That’s more greenhouse gas reduction than if they switched their servers to renewable energies.
