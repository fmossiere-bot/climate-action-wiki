###### 6.6% of the household Carbon Footprint is linked to the furtinture, painting and so on that we use inside our houses

These are the things we don't always think about. But ultiately they can matter and this s the trend we are seeing in France at the moent, with an explosion in second hand furniture for instance, and more alternative material components:
- Eco-friendly paint and wall paper - remove some of the air polluant, and lower quality materials
- floor material with focus on "where is comes from" (produced in France)
- Use of more eco-material, including textile, in all the rooms
