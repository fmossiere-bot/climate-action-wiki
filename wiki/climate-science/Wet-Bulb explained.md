What is a wet-bulb? 
**Wet-bulb temperature**

![[Screenshot 2023-09-27 at 18.37.04.png]]

Air and Humidity is what matters to us ... we are hot-blooded and so we need the ability to sweat and lower our body temperature. 

Basically wet-bulb are the temperature that matters to understand the limit the Human Body can take. 
You can us the Probably Future maps to get a better understanding of the scenario and probablity of those wet-bulb days depending on the Degree increase. 

See [Probable Future map](https://probablefutures.org/maps/?volume=heat&selected_map=days_above_28c_wet-bulb&map_version=latest&warming_scenario=1.5&map_projection=mercator#2.2/15.86/8.68)
