# **OZONE Layer Damaging**

We know that CFC gaz are responsible for damaging the ozone layer which results in warming effect on the planet. A ban on CFC was introduced decades ago and its amount has been declining. Now the second most abundant CFC, CFC-11, has been rising since 2013, and appears to come from illegal production in China. 

-   **65,000** tonnes of CFCs leak to the atmosphere every year. **China** alone seems to be responsible for 10,000 tonnes a year, as well as 40-60% of the rise in CFC-11 production
-   in 2020 it reached record high abundance in the atmosphere
-   This should be an early warning for countries to crack down on illicit or negligent production
-   We know that some are produced as a bi-product of manufacturing HFC, which is a replacement for CFC, but the rise can't be explained by this alone.
- ==Under the Montreal Protocol, their production was phased out from 1989 to 2010==


*Source: New Scientist 15th April 2023*
