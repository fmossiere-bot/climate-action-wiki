		As discussed in [[Class 7 - Mental Models]], [En-ROADS](https://www.climateinteractive.org/en-roads/) is “a freely-available online simulator that provides policymakers, educators, businesses, the media, and the public with the ability to test and explore cross-sector climate solutions.”
- En-ROADS operates within a systems thinking and multisolving model. 

There are more simulation tools you can explore by visiting the [Climate Interactive](https://www.climateinteractive.org/) website.
