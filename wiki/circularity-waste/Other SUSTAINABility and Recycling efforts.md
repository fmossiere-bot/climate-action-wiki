# Eco-Skis
New generations of skis are already on the slopes. T**==his winter, Rossignol lead the way with Essential, a recyclable ski, 77% recyclable, or 10x==** more than others. 
- Certain components, like glass Fiber, are bad for the environment. 
- An estimated **1500 tonnes** of waste linked to the ski industry in France, or 263000 pairs thrown away each year. 
- ADN Skis, a start-up, is working on 99% recyclables skis.
